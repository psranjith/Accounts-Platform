# Cold-Call / Discovery Script — Smart Accounting Reports

> 60-second opener + objection handlers. Tone: peer, not vendor. Always end on a question.

---

## 60-second opener

> "Hi {{FirstName}}, this is {{SenderName}} from **Smartsoft** — we've been in IT services for 29 years, working with CA firms and SMBs across India.
>
> The reason I'm calling — we just released **Smart Accounting Reports**, a delivery platform built on Microsoft 365 for [CA firms / business owners]. For firms, it cuts the document-collection phase of an audit from ~12 days to ~2, with a workpaper module, exception engine, and online sign-off. For owners, it puts Tally on a secure cloud and gives a live dashboard on the phone.
>
> Two-part question: are you currently feeling friction with [document chasing / month-end visibility]? And if yes, is it worth 20 minutes next week to see it on your data?"

---

## Objection handlers

### "We already use Tally on a server."
Keep your Tally. We put a secure cloud gateway in front so your team and your CA work on the same file, from anywhere, with MFA and daily backups. The Tally itself doesn't change.

### "We don't have budget."
Most firms start with the ₹1,499/user/month Tally Hosting bundle and add Audit + Power BI later. You can pilot one client engagement free, end-to-end, so you see ROI before you commit.

### "Our staff won't change tools."
That's the point. Nothing changes inside Tally. The platform sits around it — inbox, workpapers, dashboards, sign-off. Your seniors keep working in Tally; the platform handles everything else.

### "Is it safe?"
It runs in your own Microsoft 365 tenancy. Entra ID is the only login. Row-level security in Dataverse and Power BI. SharePoint document store. No third-party auth. Same security posture as your Outlook.

### "We tried portals before — clients never logged in."
They don't have to. Documents come in via WhatsApp, email and Telegram — already linked to the right client and engagement. The portal is for your team.

### "Send me an email."
Sure — I'll send the 1-page brief and the pricelist. Quick check: which is more useful for your context, the architecture brief or a 3-minute video of the audit workflow?

### "We use [other tool / in-house]."
Got it. What does it do well, and what's the one thing that still costs you time every week? *(Listen. Then map to one specific pillar — exception engine, sign-off trail, WhatsApp ingest, Power BI per company.)*

---

## Discovery questions (if call extends)

1. How many client companies / engagements per quarter?
2. Where do documents arrive today — email, WhatsApp, drives?
3. How does workpaper review happen — Excel? Tally? Email chains?
4. Who signs off, and how is it recorded?
5. What's your current dashboard / MIS situation for owners?
6. What does month-end look like — calm or chaotic?
7. Who else would be on a 20-min demo — Partner, IT, Senior?

---

## Soft close

> "Based on what you've shared, the highest-leverage thing to show you is [WhatsApp ingest / audit module / Power BI per company]. I can do that in 20 minutes — Tuesday 11 or Wednesday 4. Which works?"
