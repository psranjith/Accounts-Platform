## Thread (6 tweets) — "Why audits run late"

1/ A thread on why Indian audits run late, and what we did about it.
   We built Smart Accounting Reports for CA firms tired of chasing PDFs.

2/ Cause #1: document collection. The first 12 days of a typical statutory audit are spent collecting bank statements, ledgers, GSTRs and notices over email + WhatsApp.

3/ Cause #2: re-keying. Whatever arrives gets manually filed and tagged. Junior staff become couriers, not auditors. Quality drops, time slips.

4/ Cause #3: review bottleneck. Workpapers live in Excel. Sign-off is print → scan → email. Nobody has a single, current view of "what's pending".

5/ What we changed:
   - WhatsApp / email / Telegram → SharePoint, auto-filed to the right client
   - Workpaper checklist generated when engagement opens
   - Online sign-off, immutable audit trail
   - Exception engine runs nightly on real data
