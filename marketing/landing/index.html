Questions we hear most

Do you supply the Tally licences?

No. Tally Prime / TallyPrime Server licences are client-supplied. We host them on the Azure VM and manage everything around them.

What's the minimum term?

12 months from go-live. User count is revisable upward at any time; downward at the next quarter.

What if we need more than 25 users?

The standard D4s v5 VM is comfortable up to ~25 concurrent users. Larger user counts get a re-quote with a bigger Azure VM SKU.

Is GST included?

No. All amounts are ex GST. 18% IGST is applied on the final invoice.

What happens if Azure pricing changes?

Azure pricing is indicative as of issue date and locked at PO. A >5% movement in Microsoft list price or USD/INR FX may trigger a refresh — communicated in advance.

Can we keep our existing Tally data?

Yes. Migration of existing companies — copy, integrity verification, cut-over plan — is part of the one-time setup (waived during the introductory offer).

How is the RDS CAL pricing so much lower?

Microsoft RDS User CAL via SPLA is ₹350/user list. Under SmartSoft's hosted-Tally bundle, we pass through 50% of that — ₹175/user. It's not transferable outside the bundle.

Who is SmartSoft?

An IT services company with 29 years of delivery to Indian SMBs and CA firms. Tally on Cloud is our managed Azure hosting offer; Smart Accounting Reports is our companion delivery platform.
