Assumptions & notes

  • Tally Prime / TallyPrime Server licences are client-supplied and not included.
  • GST (18% IGST) extra on all line items.
  • Azure pricing is indicative as of issue date; final quotation locked at PO. > 5% movement in list price or USD/INR FX may trigger a refresh.
  • Minimum term: 12 months from go-live. Per-user count revisable upward at any time; downward at the next quarter.
  • Base sizing (D4s v5) is comfortable up to ~25 concurrent users. Larger user counts require a re-quote with a bigger VM.
  • The 50% RDS bundle rate is offered under SmartSoft's hosted-Tally package and is not transferable.
