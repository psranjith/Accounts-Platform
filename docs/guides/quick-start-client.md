# Quick Start — Client (Business Owner / Finance Head)

Welcome. The platform gives you one place to see your audit status, your
dashboards, and anything your accountant needs from you — scoped to **your
company only**. You don't need to learn accounting software; most of what you do
is review and the occasional upload. For exact permissions see
[../page-role-matrix.md](../page-role-matrix.md).

---

## Before you start

- Sign in with the Microsoft account from your invitation email (one secure
  login).
- Your landing page is your **Dashboard**, showing only your company.

To explore without real data, open the [preview harness](../preview-harness.md)
and set the **Role** dropdown to **Client**.

---

## 1. See your numbers

1. Open **Dashboard** for the headline view, or **Reports / Insights**
   ([insights.liquid](../power-platform/portal/pages/insights.liquid)) for the
   full Power BI dashboards — cashflow, receivables, GST, payroll.
2. These work on your phone. You see only your company's data (enforced by
   row-level security).

## 2. Respond to requests from your accountant

1. Open **Client requests**
   ([client-requests.liquid](../power-platform/portal/pages/client-requests.liquid)).
2. Each item shows what's needed and by when.
3. **Upload** the document — from the portal, or simply reply on WhatsApp and it
   arrives automatically.

## 3. Share documents

- Open **Documents**
  ([documents.liquid](../power-platform/portal/pages/documents.liquid)) to view
  or upload files for your company. You can upload and download your own
  company's documents.

## 4. Track your audit

- Open **Audit** ([audit.liquid](../power-platform/portal/pages/audit.liquid))
  to see the status of your engagement — what stage it's at and what's
  outstanding (read-only).

## 5. Manage your subscription

- Open **Billing / Subscription**
  ([billing.liquid](../power-platform/portal/pages/billing.liquid)) to see your
  current plan and upgrade if you need more (`Flow_ManageSubscription`).

---

## What you can and can't do

- ✅ View your dashboards, audit status, and documents.
- ✅ Upload documents and respond to requests.
- ✅ Change your own subscription and edit limited profile fields.
- ❌ You won't see other companies, internal workpapers, or firm-wide data —
  the platform scopes everything to you.

## Tips

- The fastest way to send a document is to **reply on WhatsApp** — no login
  needed.
- If you can't see something you expect, your accountant may not have shared it
  yet — ask them.
